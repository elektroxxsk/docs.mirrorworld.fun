# Fiat-to-Crypto

## What does Fiat-to-Crypto Solution do?

Fiat-to-Crypto solution ****facilitates seamless conversion between fiat currencies and cryptocurrencies, providing users with a smooth and streamlined experience when transacting or interacting with blockchain-based applications.

## What are the features of Fiat-to-Crypto Solution?

- **Support for multiple currencies**: Fiat-to-Crypto supports various fiat currencies, enabling users from different regions to participate in the cryptocurrency ecosystem without facing currency-related barriers.
- **Integration with multiple payment gateways**: The feature provides compatibility with a wide range of payment gateways, ensuring a convenient and accessible experience for users across the globe.
